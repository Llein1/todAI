# todAI Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 9, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 9, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to todAI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Task Data
- **Task titles and descriptions** you create
- **Dates, priorities, and categories** you assign
- **Sub-tasks** you create
- **Tags** you add to tasks
- This data is stored **locally on your device** using SQLite database

### 2. AI Processing Data
When you use AI-powered features:
- **Text you type** in the task input field
- **Task descriptions** you send for AI suggestions
- This data is sent to **Google Gemini API** (or OpenAI API if configured) for processing

### 3. Usage Data
- **Streak information** (days active, tasks completed)
- **App preferences** and settings
- This data is stored **locally on your device**

### 4. Data We DO NOT Collect
- ❌ We do not collect your personal identity information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ We do not track your location
- ❌ We do not collect device identifiers
- ❌ We do not use analytics or tracking services

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
- **All task data** is stored locally on your device
- **Database operations** happen entirely on your device
- **No server storage** - we don't have servers to store your data

### AI Features
When you use AI-powered features (optional):
- Your **task text** is sent to Google Gemini API or OpenAI API
- The AI service processes your text to:
  - Complete sentences
  - Extract dates and times
  - Suggest priorities
  - Generate sub-tasks
- **We do not store** the data sent to or received from AI services
- AI providers may process data according to their own privacy policies:
  - [Google Gemini Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All data is stored in a **SQLite database** on your device
- Data is **not backed up** to cloud services by default
- If you uninstall the app, **all data is deleted**

### API Key Security
- Your API keys are stored in the app's **secure local storage**
- API keys are **not transmitted** to any server except the respective AI service
- **You control** which AI service to use (Gemini vs OpenAI)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Control Over AI Features
- ✅ You can **choose not to use** AI features
- ✅ You can **disable AI suggestions** in settings
- ✅ You can **remove your API key** at any time
- ✅ All core features work **without AI**

### Data Deletion
- ✅ You can **delete individual tasks** at any time
- ✅ You can **clear all data** from app settings
- ✅ Uninstalling the app **deletes all local data**

### Data Portability
- ✅ Your data is stored in a **standard SQLite format**
- ✅ You can **export your data** (future feature)
- ✅ You can **backup your database** manually

## Third-Party Services

### Google Gemini API (Optional)
If you choose to use Gemini AI features:
- Your task text is sent to Google's servers
- Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You can opt-out by removing your API key

### OpenAI API (Optional)
If you choose to use OpenAI features:
- Your task text is sent to OpenAI's servers
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/privacy/
- You can opt-out by removing your API key
